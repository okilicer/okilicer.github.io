Biography

Dr. Orsan Kilicer is a Ph.D. Candidate at the Department of Mathematics, Texas A&M University.

Dr. Kilicer has received several awards for his research and teaching.

Teaching

Dr. Kilicer currently teaches courses in machine learning, data analysis, and mathematical modeling. His teaching philosophy emphasizes practical, hands-on learning and critical thinking. He is committed to helping students develop both the theoretical and practical skills necessary to excel in modern mathematical and computational fields.
