Surface Stokes: PhD Thesis Results

In my PhD thesis, I investigated the mathematical and numerical properties of the Surface Stokes problem, which models fluid flow constrained to curved surfaces. This research contributes to understanding how tangential flows behave on manifolds and their coupling with surface geometry.

Some key results include:

  • Derivation of well-posed variational formulations for the Surface Stokes equations.
  • Development of finite element methods on triangulated surfaces.
  • Convergence analysis and numerical validation on benchmark problems.
